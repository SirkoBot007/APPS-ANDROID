DayFlow is a clean, fast task and habit manager. Create tasks with a priority and due date, check them off, and keep your day organized.

KEY FEATURES
• Create, edit and delete tasks (swipe to delete).
• Priorities (low, medium, high) and an optional due date.
• Filters: All, Today, Pending and Done.
• Pending and completed counters.
• Material 3 interface with light/dark themes and subtle animations.

PRIVACY
DayFlow uses no internet and collects no data. Everything is stored only on your device.

Built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose. A Sirko007 portfolio project.
