CryptoPulse shows live prices for top cryptocurrencies, with 24h change and a 7-day trend sparkline. Tap any coin to see its details.

KEY FEATURES
• Top coins with price and 24h change.
• 7-day trend chart (sparkline) per coin.
• Pull to refresh; prices also update in the background.
• Local cache: the latest data shows instantly, even offline.
• Modern dark Material 3 interface.

DATA
Prices come from the public CoinGecko API. CryptoPulse needs no account and collects no personal data. Content is informational only and not financial advice.

Built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose. A Sirko007 portfolio project.
