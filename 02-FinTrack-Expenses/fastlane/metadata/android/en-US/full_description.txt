FinTrack is a simple expense and income tracker that works fully offline. Log your transactions, see your balance instantly, and understand where your money goes with a category breakdown chart.

KEY FEATURES
• Add income and expenses with a category (food, transport, leisure, etc.).
• Balance, total income and total expenses computed automatically.
• Donut chart with your spending broken down by category.
• Clean Material 3 interface with light and dark themes.

PRIVACY
FinTrack uses no internet and collects no data. Everything is stored only on your device.

Built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose. A Sirko007 portfolio project.
